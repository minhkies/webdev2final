Swiss Posters for the PROMS

This is one of my favourite project from Vector Graphic class. I was asked to create two Swiss-styled posters for an musical event - the Prom. Each poster can only use either a Sans-Serif or Serif typeface. Usually, sans-serif will give a modern look and feel while serif will be more classical. I chose to do the opposite way. I designed a vintages style for my sans-serif and a modern style for my serif.
